Method
  1. 1Season salmon fillet with salt and a touch of oil. Bring grill or pan to high heat.
  2. 2Cook salmon 2–3 minutes each side for medium rare — internal temp 50–55°C. The flesh should remain translucent in the centre. Rest briefly.
  3. 3Portion 200 g seasoned poke rice into bowl and press gently.
  4. 4Arrange toppings in distinct sections: red cabbage, edamame, pickled cucumber, radish julienne, seaweed salad, and cherry tomato halves.
  5. 5Slice or break salmon into portions and place on top of rice. Serve immediately without additional sauce — the salmon speaks for itself.
Plating & Service Notes
Maximum colour contrast is key. Place each topping in its own zone. Salmon placed centrally or at 12 o'clock. No additional sauce required — salmon carries the bowl. Garnish with sesame seeds.
