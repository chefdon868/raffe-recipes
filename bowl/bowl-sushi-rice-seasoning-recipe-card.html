Seasoning Mix
Rice Vinegar litre 2.25
White Sugar g 562.5
Method
  1. 1 Combine rice vinegar and white sugar in a saucepan. Stir over gentle heat until sugar fully dissolves. Do not boil. Remove from heat and allow to cool slightly.
  2. 2 Spread freshly cooked hot rice into a large flat pan (23 × 33 cm / 9 × 13"). Drizzle half the seasoning evenly over the surface.
  3. 3 Using a rice paddle or rubber spatula, cut through the rice with a folding action — do not stir or mash. Fold and turn to distribute seasoning evenly.
  4. 4 After 1 minute of folding, drizzle remaining seasoning over rice. Continue folding for a further 1 minute. Rice will appear wet — this is correct.
  5. 5 Cool rice to warm or room temperature before use — approximately 20 minutes. Use a fan to speed cooling if available. Do not refrigerate while hot.
Plating / Service Notes
Use as base for all BOWL poke recipes. Season while rice is hot. Cool to room temp before portioning.
