Method
  1. 1CRITICAL: Use sashimi-grade yellowfin tuna only. Keep refrigerated at ≤4°C until the moment of service.
  2. 2Dice tuna into uniform 5 mm cubes. Work quickly on a chilled board. Keep cold throughout prep.
  3. 3Portion 200 g seasoned poke rice into bowl and press gently.
  4. 4Arrange toppings in distinct sections: pickled cucumber, edamame, seaweed salad, julienne radish, and spring onion batons.
  5. 5Place diced tuna prominently on top of the rice. Garnish with toasted sesame seeds. Serve immediately — do not hold.
Plating & Service Notes
Tuna must be placed last and served immediately. Temperature food safety is critical — raw fish held at ≤4°C. Sesame seeds over tuna for visual finish. Do not sauce unless requested.
