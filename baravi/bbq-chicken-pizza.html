Ingredients — 1 × 12" Pizza
  • Pizza base 12" (Bakels)1 base
  • Tomato napolitana sauce100 g
  • Mozzarella, shredded140 g
  • Chicken breast, grilled & diced see prep sheet80 g
  • Onion, sliced30 g
  • Capsicum, sliced30 g
  • Mushrooms, sliced30 g
  • BBQ sauce swirl over top30 g
Method
  1. 1Preheat: Preheat pizza oven to 280°C.
  2. 2Sauce: Spread tomato napolitana sauce leaving a 2 cm border.
  3. 3Cheese: Scatter shredded mozzarella evenly.
  4. 4Toppings: Distribute grilled chicken, onion, capsicum and mushrooms evenly.
  5. 5BBQ: Swirl BBQ sauce over the toppings in a zigzag pattern.
  6. 6Bake: Bake 12–14 min until golden and bubbling. Slice into 6.
Plating Notes
BBQ swirl visible on top. Slice into 6 even pieces.
